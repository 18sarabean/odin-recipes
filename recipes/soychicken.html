Soy Chicken

Description

Soy Chicken is an easy and delicious meal that your entire family will love. Break out that crockpot and lets get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 T garlic powder
  • 1 t parsley flakes
  • 1 t onion powder
  • 1 cup water
