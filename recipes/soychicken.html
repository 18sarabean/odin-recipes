Soy Chicken

Description

Soy Chicken is an easy and delicious meal that your entire family will love. Break out that crockpot and lets get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 T garlic powder
  • 1 t parsley flakes
  • 1 t onion powder
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Spray a crockpot with nonstick spray and turn on low.
  2. Layer the chicken on the botton of the crockpot.
  3. Mix together the remaining ingredients and mix until combined.
  4. Pour the mixture over the chicken.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Put the lid on the crockpot and cook for 6 hours.
