Waffles

Description

This is a Bean Family favorite. These waffles are light, crispy and melt in your mouth delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup flour
  • 4 T cornmeal
  • 1 t baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 t salt
  • 1 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup oil

Steps

  1. Separate egg yolks from whites.Beat whites until they form stiff peaks.
  2. Combine egg yolds and other wet ingredients and mix together.
  3. Combine dry ingredients
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry and mix well.
  5. Add egg whites and stir until combined.
  6. Pour into hot waffle iron and cook.
