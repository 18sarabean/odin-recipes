Bread

Description

Not only is this bread a delight to eat, it is a delight to make. You will return to this recipe time and time again as your go-to bread recipe for all occasions.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 T yeast
  • 9 T sugar
  • 9 cups white flour
  • 3 t salt
  • 3 T shortening
